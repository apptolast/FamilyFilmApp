Fliksy helps friends, couples and families answer the same question faster: what should we watch tonight?

When a group tries to choose a movie or TV series, everyone brings different tastes, watchlists and “I’ve already seen that” moments. Fliksy turns that messy decision into a shared watchlist and recommendation flow built for movie nights.

Create a group, save films and series, mark what each person has watched or wants to watch, and reveal picks that make sense for everyone. Instead of jumping between streaming apps, notes and group chats, keep the decision in one place.

What you can do with Fliksy:

- Discover popular movies and TV series.
- Search and save titles to your personal lists.
- Create groups for family, friends, couples or flatmates.
- Track what each person has watched, skipped or wants to see.
- Reveal group recommendations when it is time to choose.
- Use Chat Premium to ask for movie and TV recommendations.
- Remove ads with a one-time purchase.

Use Fliksy for family film nights, weekend plans, shared watchlists, date nights and any moment when “what should we watch?” takes too long.

Fliksy is designed for people who love films and series, but do not want to spend the whole evening scrolling.
